About Us

Recruiting as we know it today is broken and ripe for disruption. Employers are not getting the talent they need and if they do, it is a blow to the bottom line. Candidates are not getting a response; crickets. They submit their resume/CV and then everything goes dark.

Launch and Hire is different.

Here at Launch and Hire, we are changing that narrative.
We are about engagement - on both sides. We build relationships. We connect with employers to gain a good understanding of the type of person that is required, and then we get straight to work.

We connect and stay connected.

For candidates, the same process is followed. We connect and stay connected. They will hear from us on a regular basis. They will know exactly what is going on, and whether or not they are still being considered. We live by the philosophy of 'treat others how you want to be treated'.
